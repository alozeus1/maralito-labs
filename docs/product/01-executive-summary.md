# 01 · Executive Summary

## Product summary
**BorderPass** is a premium cross-border shopping concierge for customers in **Ciudad Juárez** who want to buy from **U.S. stores without crossing the border**. A customer asks BorderPass to buy an item (or ships their own purchase to the **BorderPass El Paso Hub**), uploads receipts and border/import information, receives a **transparent quote** (service fee + item value + estimated duties), pays securely, then **tracks the package's journey** from El Paso across the border to their door in Juárez — with **inspection photos**, a **human concierge**, and **delivery confirmation** along the way. It is the first app on the Maralito Platform and runs on the Maralito Automation Platform.

## Target market
Primarily **residents of Ciudad Juárez** (and the greater Juárez–El Paso border region) who shop U.S. retail but face friction crossing: people **without a U.S. visa**, busy parents and families, students, frequent cross-border shoppers, and **small businesses** procuring U.S. goods. Initial wedge: Juárez consumers who already buy from U.S. stores but rely on informal arrangements (friends/family crossing, ad-hoc forwarders, personal shoppers) and want something **trustworthy, transparent, and premium**.

## Core problem
Buying from U.S. stores while living in Juárez is **slow, opaque, and risky**:
- Crossing the border costs hours and often requires a **visa many people don't have**.
- Existing options are fragmented — informal couriers, mail-forwarders, and personal shoppers — with **hidden fees, unclear duties, no inspection, no real tracking, and weak accountability**.
- Customers can't easily see **what they'll pay** (item + service + import duties) or **trust** that the right item arrived undamaged and legitimately cleared customs.

## BorderPass solution
A single, premium concierge that handles the **entire cross-border path** end-to-end:
- **Buy-for-me, package reception, local pickup, and business delivery** services (per the Stitch home + new-request screens).
- A **transparent quote** with AI-assisted duty estimation before the customer commits (`HUMAN-APPROVAL` for risky/high-value).
- **Secure payment** (Stripe via the platform), **physical inspection with photos + serial/seal verification** at the El Paso Hub, and a **signature vertical "Border Journey"** that narrates every stage from Purchased → Delivered.
- A **human concierge** (WhatsApp/chat) layered over AI agents that do the heavy lifting, so customers feel **personal care with logistics-grade reliability**.

## Why now
- **Persistent border friction** and visa constraints make crossing for shopping costly; demand for U.S. goods in Juárez is structural, not seasonal.
- **Cross-border e-commerce and "buy-for-me"/forwarding models** have matured globally, proving willingness to pay for trusted intermediaries. `⚠️ VERIFY` current market sizing with primary research before scaling spend.
- **AI agents + durable automation** (the Maralito Automation Platform) now make it feasible to run intake, risk, quoting, and coordination at low marginal cost while keeping humans on the decisions that matter.
- A **premium, trust-first brand** ("Warm Professionalism") is differentiated in a category dominated by commodity couriers and informal services.

## Business opportunity
- **Revenue:** transparent **service fee per order** (Stitch shows a $15 base for Buy-for-Me) plus potential margin on duties handling, premium/VIP tiers, business/procurement plans, and future marketplace/subscription/returns services (per the product spec V2/V3).
- **Defensibility grows with trust + data:** inspection records, customs handling, concierge relationships, and a reusable platform create switching costs and operating leverage.
- **Expansion paths:** more border corridors, more service types (returns, subscriptions), and B2B procurement — all reusing the same platform and automation.

## Strategic advantage
1. **Trust as the product** — inspection photos, serial/seal verification, transparent duties, money-back/insurance trust cards, and a named human concierge.
2. **Platform leverage** — auth, payments, notifications, files, AI, audit, and automation are inherited from Maralito, so BorderPass focuses on the cross-border experience, not plumbing.
3. **AI-native operations with human judgment** — agents handle intake/risk/quoting/coordination; humans approve compliance- and money-sensitive calls.
4. **Premium bilingual brand** purpose-built for the Juárez–El Paso corridor (EN/ES first-class, culturally resonant warmth).

## MVP objective
Prove that a Juárez customer can **complete one trustworthy buy-for-me / receive-my-package order end-to-end** — request → (manual) risk review → transparent quote → Stripe payment → El Paso Hub reception → inspection with photos → border crossing tracking → delivery confirmation — with **human concierge support** and **full audit logging**, using **mostly manual operations behind an automated, polished customer experience**. Success = customers trust it enough to **pay and reorder**, and operations can run it reliably at pilot volume.

## Long-term vision
BorderPass becomes the **trusted digital bridge between U.S. retailers and Mexican customers** — not a delivery app, but the default, premium way to shop across the border. Over time: full AI shopping assistant (URL→quote with duty estimation), business procurement, marketplace integration, subscription shopping, U.S.-store returns, and expansion to additional border corridors — each built on the same Maralito platform and automation fabric, so new capabilities and even new markets launch quickly and safely.
